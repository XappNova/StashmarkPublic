Getting Started

How do I enable the Safari extension?

Open Safari ‚Üí Settings (‚åò,) ‚Üí Extensions tab ‚Üí Check "Stashmark Extension" to enable it. You'll see the Stashmark icon appear in Safari's toolbar.

How do I save a bookmark?

Click the Stashmark icon in Safari's toolbar while viewing any webpage. The extension will capture the page title and URL. Add tags, select a category, and click Save.

How do I access my saved bookmarks?

Open the Stashmark app from your Applications folder or Launchpad. All your saved bookmarks are displayed in the main window, organized by categories.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do AI suggestions work?

Stashmark uses Apple's Natural Language framework to analyze page titles and suggest relevant categories and tags. All processing happens on your device‚Äîno data is sent to external servers.

Can I create custom categories?

Yes! Click the folder icon with a plus in the toolbar, or right-click in the sidebar and select "New Category." You can customize the name, icon, and color for each category.

How do I organize categories hierarchically?

When creating or editing a category, select a "Parent Category" to nest it under another category. You can create multi-level folder structures just like in Finder.

Can I reorder my bookmarks and categories?

Yes! Simply drag and drop bookmarks within a list to reorder them. Categories in the sidebar can also be dragged to change their order.

How do I search my bookmarks?

Use the search field in the sidebar. You can search by title, URL, or tags. Type #tagname to search specifically by tag.

Can I import bookmarks from other browsers?

Yes! Go to the menu ‚Üí Import. Stashmark supports standard HTML bookmark files (exported from Chrome, Firefox, Safari) and its own JSON format.

How do I export my bookmarks?

Go to the menu ‚Üí Export. Your bookmarks will be saved as a JSON file in your Downloads folder. You can use this for backup or transferring to another Mac.

Do my bookmarks sync across devices?

Yes, if you have iCloud enabled. Stashmark uses CloudKit to sync your bookmarks across all your Macs signed into the same Apple ID.

Troubleshooting

The Safari extension isn't showing

Go to Safari ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Extensions and ensure "Stashmark Extension" is checked. If it's not listed, try restarting Safari or your Mac.

Bookmarks aren't syncing

Check that you're signed into iCloud on all devices. Go to System Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí iCloud and ensure iCloud Drive is enabled. Give it a few minutes for changes to sync.

The extension says "Cannot connect to app"

Make sure the main Stashmark app has been launched at least once. The extension needs the app to be initialized to share data. Launch Stashmark from your Applications folder.

My categories disappeared

Check if you're looking at a filtered view (Favorites, Unread, etc.). Click "All Links" in the sidebar to see everything. If data is truly missing, check your iCloud sync status or restore from a Time Machine backup.

Import isn't working

Ensure your file is in a supported format (JSON or HTML). For HTML files, use the standard Netscape bookmark format that browsers export. Large files may take a moment to process.
